A message from our CEO

Hana serves a global market covering a diverse range of industries. Our leading edge capabilities play an important role for the automotive, mobile communications, IoT, medical, computing, industrial & consumer markets. Whilst we continually invest in leading edge technologies — the real key to our success are our team of highly skilled professionals in many disciplines that support our growth. We have over 10,000 staff but we pride ourselves in maintaining a family management style with a strong focus on the wellbeing of our employees and developing them to their full potential.

If you think you can contribute and have the ability — why not consider joining the 'Hana Family' to see how far you can go in playing your part in the fast growing world of microelectronics?

Richard Han CEO of Hana Group
